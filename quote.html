Important FAQ

Does your product has any qualification?

All of our Ace Elevators Products are designed and manufactured to be in compliance and certified with ISO 9001:2008 standard.

What is your warranty policy?

Most of the parts will be covered by the 12 months warranty.

What type of lifts does Ace Elevators Pvt Ltd provide?

Ace Elevators Pvt Ltd provides wide selection of lifts including: construction lifts, material lifts, work platform, hydrulic lifts and good lifts etc. We offer the regular size and customized size of Elevators and Esclators.

Do Ace Elevators have oversea service?

We provide oversea service support which include installation support, maintenance and part replacement, training and technical support.

When elevator closed will cause harm?

In the elevator door closing process, if people or things touch the hall door, the door of elevator will automatically restart, and it’s safe. Because the door is provided with anti clamping switch, once the gate to touch the person or thing, this switching action make the elevator can not closed, and reopened, and then shut the door. In addition, closing force is required, and it will not hurt person.
